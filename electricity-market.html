Electricity Market

Research on electricity markets, pricing, and optimization

Research Summary

Research in electricity markets focuses on applying optimization, stochastic methods, and AI techniques to enhance market design, pricing mechanisms, and grid operation. Dr. Pirnia's work in this area draws on his experience at the California ISO and FERC, addressing challenges in wholesale and retail electricity markets, renewable integration, and demand response.
